--- Method: Hierarchical Chunking ---
Description: Splits documents into "Parents" (Large Context) and "Children" (Precise Match).
Parameters: chunk_sizes (list of integers)
Concept: "Index the Child, Retrieve the Parent." (Small chunks search better; Big chunks answer better).

[Mechanism Flow]
1. Ingestion: We save BOTH Parent and Child nodes. Child nodes link to Parent ID.
2. Search: We search against the SMALL Child nodes (more precise).
3. Retrieval: If a Child matches, we swap it for its PARENT node to give the LLM full context.

Number of Chunks: Multiple levels (Parent + Children)

  Node ID: `Parent_1`
